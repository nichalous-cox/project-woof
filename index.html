Aussie Cow Doggo

Doggo Facts

  • They are an extremely intelligent, active, and sturdy dog breed.
  • Developed by Australian settlers to handle herds of cattle on expansive ranches, they still used today as a herding dog.
  • They thrives on having a job to do and on being part of all family activities.
  • They are loyal and protective of his family, though wary of outsiders.

Dog Activity level

They are a high-energy working dog. They are not couch potatos. They want to be active and busy most of the time. Their energy must be directed, or they'll become bored and will resort to entertaining theirselves, usually by doing something you consider naughty, like digging in the trash or digging up your yard.
